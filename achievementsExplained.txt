FLAVORTOWN ACHIEVEMENTS GUIDE
==============================

This guide explains how to earn each achievement in Flavortown, including hidden and secret achievements.
Achievements may award cookie rewards upon completion!

================================================================================
VISIBLE ACHIEVEMENTS
================================================================================

1. Anyone Can Cook! [first_login]
   Icon: chepheus
   Description: welcome to the kitchen, chef
   How to Earn: Simply create an account and log in for the first time
   Cookie Reward: 0 cookies

2. Very Fried [identity_verified]
   Icon: verified
   Description: prove you belong in this kitchen!
   How to Earn: Complete the identity verification process and get verified
   Cookie Reward: 5 cookies

3. Home Cookin' [first_project]
   Icon: fork_spoon_fill
   Description: fire up the stove and start your first dish
   How to Earn: Create your first project in Flavortown
   Cookie Reward: 3 cookies

4. Recipe Notes [first_devlog]
   Icon: edit
   Description: jot down your cooking process
   How to Earn: Create your first devlog post on any of your projects
   Cookie Reward: 2 cookies

5. Yapper [first_comment]
   Icon: rac_yap
   Description: awawawawawawawa
   How to Earn: Post your first comment anywhere on the site
   Cookie Reward: 0 cookies

6. Off the Menu [first_order]
   Icon: shopping_cart_1_fill
   Description: treat yourself to something from the shop
   How to Earn: Make your first shop purchase (excluding free stickers)
   Cookie Reward: 0 cookies

7. Regular Customer [five_orders]
   Icon: shopping
   Description: 5 orders in - the kitchen knows your name now
   How to Earn: Complete 5 real shop orders
   Progress Tracking: Shows X/5 orders completed
   Cookie Reward: 0 cookies

8. VIP Diner [ten_orders]
   Icon: shopping_cart_1_fill
   Description: 10 orders?! we're naming a dish after you
   How to Earn: Complete 10 real shop orders
   Progress Tracking: Shows X/10 orders completed
   Cookie Reward: 0 cookies

9. Line Cook [five_projects]
   Icon: square_fill
   Description: 5 dishes cooking at once? mise en place!
   How to Earn: Create 5 projects
   Progress Tracking: Shows X/5 projects created
   Cookie Reward: 10 cookies

10. Order Up! [first_ship]
    Icon: ship
    Description: ship your first project to the world
    How to Earn: Submit your first project with ship_status set to "submitted"
    Cookie Reward: 3 cookies

11. Michelin Star [ship_certified]
    Icon: trophy
    Description: your dish has been certified by the critics
    How to Earn: Have one of your ship events get approved (certification_status = "approved")
    Cookie Reward: 3 cookies

================================================================================
ADDITIONAL SECRET ACHIEVEMENTS
================================================================================

Note: There may be additional secret achievements defined in the private secrets 
submodule that are not publicly documented here. These achievements are only 
available in production and are kept secret to maintain their surprise factor!

To check for these achievements:
- The system loads secret achievements from SecretAchievements::DEFINITIONS
- These are defined in the secrets submodule (secrets/app/models/)
- They are only available when the secrets submodule is present
- Watch for achievement notifications as you use the platform!

================================================================================
ACHIEVEMENT SYSTEM DETAILS
================================================================================

Cookie Rewards:
- Some achievements grant cookie rewards when earned
- Cookies are automatically added to your balance via ledger entries
- The reward amount is shown when you earn the achievement

Progress Tracking:
- Some achievements show your progress (e.g., "3/5 orders completed")
- Progress is visible for achievements that track cumulative actions
- Secret achievements don't show progress until earned

Visibility Types:
- VISIBLE: Name, description, and icon are always shown
- SECRET: Shows as "???" until earned, may show progress
- HIDDEN: Completely hidden until earned, no progress shown

Achievement Notifications:
- You'll see a notification when you earn an achievement
- Notifications show the achievement name, description, icon, and cookie reward
- Notifications are displayed in the flash messages area

Tips for Achievement Hunters:
1. Focus on creating projects and devlogs to earn multiple achievements
2. Engage with the community through comments and interactions
3. Complete your identity verification early for the cookie boost
4. Keep an eye on progress-tracked achievements to see how close you are
5. Shop orders count toward multiple achievements
6. Secret achievements often reward creative or unexpected behavior
7. Staff-recognized "fire" projects can earn you the rare "Cooking" achievement
